COVID-19 Quarantine Management System

Through this system, the COVID-19 quarantined people can be monitored. They can update their health status daily to the Police and also they can send their requests for their daily needs. All the quarantined people who are having a smartphone can install this StayHomeLK app. Through the app, they will be connected to the Central Quarantined People Management System. The Police will use the central system to check the health status and other daily requirements of the quarantined people that use the mobile app, through the Central system.

The StayHomeLK app is available in both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store Apple App Store.

The police department will notify the person to install the app. Only then will the person in quarantine be able to install the app and register into the system by providing his or her mobile phone number.

Using this Mobile App a quarantined person can,

  • Update his or her daily health status to the relevant Police Station
  • Request their daily needs including food, medicine, and other requirements to the relevant Police Station.

Using the Central System, the relevant Police Station/Officer can

  • Monitor the health status of the people who have registered with the mobile app and are currently in quarantine, individually.
  • Receive daily requests of the mobile app users.
  • Track the person’s location.
  • Generate reports about people - daily basis, health status basis, quarantine status basis, etc.
  • Visualize mobile app user’s locations on a map.
